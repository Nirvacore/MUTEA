# Mu Tea — Business Description

*Prepared for Yunomi Wholesale Account Application*

---

Mu Tea is a mindful lifestyle brand based in Bangkok, Thailand, dedicated to bringing the art of Japanese tea culture to Southeast Asian consumers through a gentle, human-centered approach.

Our philosophy — "Void to Void" — reflects our belief that tea is not merely a product, but a bridge that helps people return to stillness in the midst of modern life's constant noise. We serve primarily women aged 28-55, burnout professionals, and mindfulness-curious individuals who are seeking calm, clarity, and connection through daily ritual rather than consumption.

We are building Mu Tea across three carefully curated collections that mirror different states of being:

- **Mu Earth** — Grounding teas for evening and calm (hojicha, deep-roasted varieties)
- **Mu Space** — Meditative teas for ritual and stillness (premium ceremonial matcha, gyokuro)
- **Mu Sun** — Bright, everyday teas for warmth and comfort (genmaicha, sencha, latte-grade matcha)

Our phased business model begins with curated Japanese tea sourced through trusted partners like Yunomi and direct farm relationships. We plan to grow through subscription boxes, followed by private-label development, workshops and tea retreats, and eventually an international community of mindful tea drinkers.

We are particularly interested in Yunomi's diverse selection of single-cultivar matcha (Samidori, Okumidori, Gokou), regional hojicha, and artisan genmaicha. We value the stories behind each farm and each tea maker — this narrative depth is central to how we communicate with our audience.

In Thailand, Japanese tea culture is rapidly growing but still largely dominated by mass-market brands and sweetened bottled tea. Mu Tea occupies a different space: we introduce our customers to the craft, the cultivars, and the quiet beauty of Japanese tea tradition. We believe this creates lasting relationships with both the tea and the people who grow it.

Our initial wholesale needs are modest — approximately 2-5 kg per month across 5-8 SKUs — as we focus on depth rather than breadth. We measure success not by volume, but by the meaningful connections we create. Our founding metric: 10 people saying, "This tea helped me return home to myself."

We look forward to building a long-term partnership with Yunomi and the farming communities it represents. Connection before commerce — always.
