# Mu Tea — Product Architecture

## Three Collections

### Mu Earth — Grounding · Evening · Calm
> For winding down

| Product | Type | Caffeine | Source | Key Attribute |
|---------|------|----------|--------|--------------|
| Gokou Matcha | Ceremonial | Moderate | Obubu/Yunomi | Deep, creamy umami |
| Hojicha | Roasted tea | Very low (7-15 mg) | Obubu/Yunomi | Toasty, warm, bedtime-safe |
| Kukicha | Stem tea | Ultra low | Yunomi | Lightest possible, gentle |

### Mu Space — Meditative · Ritual · Still
> For mindful practice

| Product | Type | Caffeine | Source | Key Attribute |
|---------|------|----------|--------|--------------|
| Samidori Ceremonial | Premium matcha | Moderate | Obubu/Yunomi | Flagship, high umami/sweetness |
| Gyokuro | Shade-grown leaf | Moderate-high | Yunomi | Deepest umami, connoisseur |
| Okumidori Ceremonial | Ceremonial matcha | Moderate | Obubu/Yunomi | Balanced, accessible ceremony |

### Mu Sun — Bright · Everyday · Warm
> For everyday ritual

| Product | Type | Caffeine | Source | Key Attribute |
|---------|------|----------|--------|--------------|
| Spring Blend Matcha | Everyday/café | Moderate | Obubu | Latte-friendly, versatile |
| Genmaicha | Rice green tea | Low (15-20 mg) | Obubu/Yunomi | Comforting, popcorn-like, accessible |
| Sencha | Classic green | Moderate | Yunomi | Daily drinking, classic |

## Caffeine Spectrum (Low → High)

```
Kukicha (3-5mg) → Hojicha (7-15mg) → Genmaicha (15-20mg) → Sencha (30-40mg)
→ Spring Blend (40-50mg) → Okumidori (50-60mg) → Gokou (50-60mg)
→ Samidori (60-70mg) → Gyokuro (70-80mg)
```

| Tier | Products | Target Price | Margin Goal |
|------|----------|-------------|-------------|
| Accessible | Genmaicha, Hojicha, Kukicha | ฿350-550 | 50-60% |

## Phase 1 Starting Lineup (5 SKUs)

1. **Samidori Ceremonial** — Flagship (Mu Space)
2. **Okumidori Ceremonial** — Accessible ceremony (Mu Space)

4. **Hojicha** — Evening/calm (Mu Earth)
